## Audit Boundary

This audit covers explicit size-only replacement of persistent `TextureResource` allocations and every currently implemented downstream consumer. General descriptor reconfiguration, automatic surface tracking, direct texture `ReadbackOperation`, device-loss rehydration, persistent logical texture-view resources, and asynchronous WebGPU error-scope policy remain separate work.
